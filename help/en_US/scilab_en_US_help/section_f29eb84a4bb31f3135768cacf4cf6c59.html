Regression tools (regtools)

  • ff2n — Defines a two level full factorial design with n factors
  • fullfact — Define a full factorial design matrix.
  • linregr — An interactive tool for carrying out multi linear regression.
  • nlinlsq — Non-linear [weighted] least square solver with statistical analysis of the solution.
  • nlinregr — Interactive non linear regression solver
  • qqplot — Draw a quantile-quantile plot
